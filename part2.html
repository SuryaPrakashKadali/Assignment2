Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known.Few have tried it and linved to tell about it,and these are their stories.
We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as aresult of consuming Magic is your responsibility.You've been warned, and you can view ourlegal pagefor more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar,and butter in alarge bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
